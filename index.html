Circumstances Beyond One's Control

Wild fires cause devastations across the world. Donate to this cause to help those in need.
Donate to those who are less fortunate than you.

People have nowhere to live, because of natural disasters. It is up to us to help them, we live our lives with such blissful ignorance. If we are blessed, and don't have to worry about this happening to our home we should help others beacuse many are not as lucky. So, help us make more people aware of others who are suffering.
